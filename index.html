Your Global Logistics Gateway

SWEN Container Line is a globally integrated shipping, transport, and logistics company, specializing in container shipping across major ports in India, Singapore, Malaysia, UAE, Bangladesh, Srilanka, Indonesia and Thailand.

With frequent and timely sailings, strategically positioned facilities, specialized high-quality equipment, and a team of highly qualified professionals, we provide reliable ocean cargo services, including temperature-controlled (reefer) transportation and over-dimensional cargo handling, ensuring your shipments are managed with the utmost care and efficiency.
