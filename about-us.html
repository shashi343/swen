About Us

Welcome to SWEN Container Line

SWEN Container Line was incorporated in 2017 at Chennai, India. Headquarterd in Singapore we have own presence in India, Singapore, Malaysia, Srilanka & Bangladesh. Started with 60 TEus, and has since expanded to key global markets connecting business across continents with current fleet size of 6000+ TEUs.

Our mission is to provide personalized and efficient logistics solutions that meet the unique needs to each of our clients with years of experience and team of experts we are dedicated to empowering your business with seamless shipping experience.
